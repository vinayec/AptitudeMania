Problems on Profit and Loss

1. Alfred buys an old scooter for Rs. 4700 and spends Rs. 800 on its repairs. If he sells the

scooter for Rs. 5800, his gain percent is:

A.447percent

B.5151percent

C.10percent

D.12percent

Answer: Option B



2. The cost price of 20 articles is the same as the selling price of x articles. If the profit is

25percent, then the value of x is:

A.15

B.16

C.18

D.25

Answer: Option B



3. If selling price is doubled, the profit triples. Find the profit percent.

A.6623

B.100

C.10513

D.120

Answer: Option B



4. In a certain store, the profit is 320percent of the cost. If the cost increases by 25percent but the selling

price remains constant, approximately what percentage of the selling price is the profit?

A.30percent

B.70percent

C.100percent

D.250percent

Answer: Option B



5. A vendor bought toffees at 6 for a rupee. How many for a rupee must he sell to gain 20percent?

A.3

B.4

C.5

D.6

Answer: Option C
