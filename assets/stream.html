Boats and Streams Important Formulas

1. Downstream/Upstream:

In water, the direction along the stream is called downstream.

And, the direction against the stream is called upstream.

2. If the speed of a boat in still water is u km/hr and

the speed of the stream is v km/hr, then:

      Speed downstream = (u + v) km/hr.

      Speed upstream = (u - v) km/hr.

3. If the speed downstream is a km/hr and

the speed upstream is b km/hr, then:

      Speed in still water =(1/2)(a + b) km/hr.

      Rate of stream =(1/2)(a - b) km/hr.
